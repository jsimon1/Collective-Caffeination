At our first group project meeting, nearly all of us came with some form of caffeinated beverage in hand, so creating a web application that makes it easier for students to meet over a cup of coffee seemed like something that many RPI students would enjoy and could relate to. By keeping this application closed to students with an @rpi.edu email address, and all hosts are checked ahead of time, attending a karafe (meeting with other students) is safe and fun.We knew we wanted to make something to help students at RPI who would not ordinarily meet, get a chance to go outside of their comfort zone and make friends on campus.
